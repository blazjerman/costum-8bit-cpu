//This instructions are based on real pins on chip.

RAM:
-enable
-read
-write 

SPM:
-enable
-pop 8 or 16
-push 8 or 16

REGAB:
-enable
-write A or B
-read A or B

ALU:
-enable
-sum 
-sub 
-not 
-or
-and
-xor 
-shl 
-shr 

JMP:
-enable
-inc by 1
-inc by 4 (POINTER) if c or not
-inc by 4 (POINTER) if z or not
-ignore c 
-ignore z 

INT: (Cpu freq must be much higher then device)
-enable
-enable or disable check
-read
-write
